# Pain Mapping

## Overview

Turns signals into useful hypotheses without pretending to know the prospect’s internal pain.

## Procedure

1. Start from a sourced signal.
2. Map the likely business implication.
3. Phrase as hypothesis, never fact.
4. Connect only to a relevant partner value.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Pain is phrased as probable/hypothesis.
- [ ] Pain traces to a signal.
- [ ] No unsupported internal claims.
