# Email Sequencing

## Overview

Prepares multi-touch email sequences. It does not send emails unless a compliant integration and human approval are present.

## Procedure

1. Use a personalized first email.
2. Draft two light follow-ups.
3. Preserve opt-out language.
4. Save to review queue with `manual_review`.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] First email uses a real signal.
- [ ] Follow-ups add value, not pressure.
- [ ] No sending without approval.
