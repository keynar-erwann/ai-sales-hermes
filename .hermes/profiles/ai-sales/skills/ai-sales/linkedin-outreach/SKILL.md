# LinkedIn Outreach

## Overview

Prepares LinkedIn messages. It does not automate LinkedIn actions by default.

## Procedure

1. Verify the person/profile source.
2. Draft a short connection or DM.
3. Keep cadence conservative.
4. Route to manual review or approved integration.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Profile is sourced.
- [ ] No scraping or automation without approved tool.
- [ ] Message is personalized.
