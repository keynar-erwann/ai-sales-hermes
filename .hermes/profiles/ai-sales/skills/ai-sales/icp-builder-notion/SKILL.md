# ICP — Notion (SaaS France)

## Partner offer
Notion as a central workspace for documentation, project management, and knowledge sharing for SaaS teams.

## Target geography
France (French-speaking SaaS ecosystem).

## Account segments
1. **The "Scaling" SaaS (15-200 employees):** Fast-growing companies post-funding, facing onboarding/knowledge chaos.
2. **"Product-Centric" Teams:** Fintech/Regtech/Deeptech building complex products needing deep documentation.
3. **"Remote-First" SaaS:** Distributed teams requiring asynchronous written alignment.

## Buyer personas
1. **CTO / VP Engineering:** Needs technical documentation centralization.
2. **Head of Product:** Needs product specifications and roadmap visibility.
3. **COO / Chief of Staff:** Needs internal processes and documentation structure.

## Trigger signals
1. **New funding (Series A/B):** Indicates rapid hiring phase (15-200 range).
2. **New leadership hire (CTO/CPO):** High intent to reorganize processes.
3. **Recruitment for "Operations" or "Onboarding" roles:** Pain signal regarding process structure.

## Pain hypotheses
- Knowledge silos prevent scaling.
- Onboarding takes too long due to fragmented docs.
- Product/Tech misalignment.

## Disqualifiers
- Enterprises > 500 employees (Notion already adopted or complex legacy).
- Very early-stage pre-seed startups (no time for docs).
- Non-digital sectors (retail, construction).

## Assumptions to validate
- Partner target is specifically the French SaaS market.
- High-intent teams are identified by scaling/recruitment patterns.
