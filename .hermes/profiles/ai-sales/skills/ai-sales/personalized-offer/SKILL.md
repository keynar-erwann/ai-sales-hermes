# Personalized Offer

## Overview

Creates a one-pager offer tailored to a specific prospect.

## Procedure

1. Use a sourced audit.
2. Pick one value proposition.
3. Write a short offer and CTA.
4. Save for review.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Offer is prospect-specific.
- [ ] CTA is low pressure.
- [ ] Claims are supported.
