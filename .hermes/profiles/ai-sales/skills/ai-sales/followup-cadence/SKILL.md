# Followup Cadence

## Overview

Plans follow-ups without spam or artificial urgency.

## Procedure

1. Read CRM stage and prior interaction.
2. Choose cadence type: silence, later, no-show, post_meeting.
3. Draft one concise next touch.
4. Respect opt-outs.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Cadence matches stage.
- [ ] Volume is conservative.
- [ ] Opt-outs excluded.
