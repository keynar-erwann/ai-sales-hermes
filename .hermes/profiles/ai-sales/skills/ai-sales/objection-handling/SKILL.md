# Objection Handling

## Overview

Drafts responses to common objections while staying human and non-pushy.

## Procedure

1. Identify the objection.
2. Acknowledge it plainly.
3. Respond with one relevant proof or clarification.
4. Offer a low-friction next step or close politely.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] No pressure.
- [ ] No unsupported claims.
- [ ] Respect opt-outs and timing.
