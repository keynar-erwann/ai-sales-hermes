# Handoff Brief

## Overview

Prepares context before a sales meeting.

## Procedure

1. Summarize account and contact.
2. Include sources, signal, pain hypothesis and prior messages.
3. List risks and unknowns.
4. Suggest meeting objective and questions.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Sources included.
- [ ] Unknowns explicit.
- [ ] Brief is concise enough to use before a call.
