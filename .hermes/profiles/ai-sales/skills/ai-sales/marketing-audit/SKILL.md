# Marketing Audit

## Overview

Creates a value-first audit from public evidence.

## Procedure

1. Inspect website/social/source.
2. Identify one strength and one opportunity.
3. Connect to the partner offer.
4. Save as an asset, not a sent message.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Audit cites sources.
- [ ] No harsh/unproven criticism.
- [ ] Clear next action.
