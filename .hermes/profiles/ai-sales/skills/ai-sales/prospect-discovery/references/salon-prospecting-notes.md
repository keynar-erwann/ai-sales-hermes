# Salon Sector Prospecting Notes

## Pain Mapping for AI Vocal Agents
- **Core pain:** "Telephone disruption" during high-value technical services (coloration, balayage) leads to both:
    - Degraded client experience (customer in the chair feels neglected).
    - Loss of revenue (busy signal, phone missed, call abandoned).
- **Secondary pain:** Difficulty standardizing the brand voice for franchises/groups.
- **Conversion angle:** Do not sell "tech". Sell "serenity for the artist" and "revenue recovery".

## Sector-Specific Search Behavior
- **Google/DuckDuckGo restrictions:** Major search engines aggressively block automated research for this sector due to volume of commercial/retail listings.
- **Bypass strategies:**
    - Use regional/local query modifiers: "meilleurs salons [ville] [spécialité]".
    - Target "Groups/Franchise" lists directly rather than individual salons.
    - Leverage "Recruitment" signals (`welcometothejungle`, job boards) as they indicate growth, which is a stronger indicator of capacity/need than mere reputation.
- **Data validation:** For salons, cross-reference the website presence with Google Maps/Review presence to ensure the establishment is active and high-traffic.
