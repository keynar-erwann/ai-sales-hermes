# Instagram Outreach

## Overview

Prepares Instagram DMs for cases where Instagram is actually relevant. No automation by default.

## Procedure

1. Verify the account is professional/relevant.
2. Use a visible content signal.
3. Draft a short human DM.
4. Route to manual review.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Account/source is public and relevant.
- [ ] DM references a real content signal.
- [ ] No automated sending.
