# Signal Detection

## Overview

Finds public, source-backed reasons why a prospect is worth contacting now or why the account is relevant.

## Procedure

1. Inspect website, news, social/public pages, job posts and reviews.
2. Record the exact source URL.
3. Classify the signal: hiring, launch, expansion, content, service focus, review pattern, training, or unknown.
4. If no source exists, mark as hypothesis and route to `research_more`.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Every signal has a source URL.
- [ ] Signal is separate from pain hypothesis.
- [ ] Unsourced signals are not used in outreach.
