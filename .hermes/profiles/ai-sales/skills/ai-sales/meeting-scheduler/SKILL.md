# Meeting Scheduler

## Overview

Transforms interest into a calendar-ready next step.

## Procedure

1. Confirm the right person and topic.
2. Propose 2-3 slots or ask for availability.
3. Add buffer/context notes.
4. Update CRM.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] No calendar action without approval/integration.
- [ ] Meeting purpose is clear.
- [ ] Handoff brief is triggered.
